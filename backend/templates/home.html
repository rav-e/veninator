Frequently Asked Questions

These are some of the most frequently asked questions on our social media panels and forums.

  • 1. Why Information such as Aadhar Number, Blood Group & Medical Condition are required ?

    Aadhar Number is used to Track the User & Medical condition is used for Vaccination purpose . Some vaccine requires your Medical Information Such as Blood Group, Allergies etc. In order to check patient doesn't get side effect of Vaccine.

  • 2. What is Process of Booking an Appointment through Veninator.

    User Creates his/her account, Fill the necessary Credentials required to check the eligibility & to keep track Selects the Vaccination centers from the Listed one, and Appoint is booked.

  • 3. Is creating Account necessary ?

    Yes. In order to keep track of patient.

  • 4. Do I have an option for cancellation of appointment?

    No. Right now Veninator doesn't support cancellation of Appointment.

  • 5. Why there are different Vaccine Listed?

    User has freedom to select which vaccine they want. All are approved by Government and are completely safe.
